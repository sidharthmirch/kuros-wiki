# Daily Review

## Purpose

Summarize the day's research movement and make tomorrow's next step obvious.

## Inputs

- Changed files from the current day.
- Active threads, open questions, drafts, and tasks.

## Outputs

- A dated review in `drafts/` or `sessions/`.
- Suggested next actions and follow-up skills.

## Invocation Expectations

1. Inspect recent files and ambient job history.
2. Group changes by thread or research question.
3. Highlight unresolved questions and stale drafts.
4. Write a concise review with provider provenance.

## Review Shape

- Today's movement
- Active threads
- New or changed sources
- Open questions
- Suggested next actions
- Follow-up skills

## Rules

- Keep it skimmable.
- Do not promote drafts to accepted notes without explicit approval.
