# Research Sprint

## Purpose

Help the user focus on one bounded research question, gather context, and produce useful next artifacts.

## Inputs

- A research question, thread, source set, or brief goal.
- Optional timebox and provider preference.

## Outputs

- Sprint plan in `tasks/` or `drafts/`.
- Notes, source captures, claims, and questions created during the sprint.
- Closeout summary when the sprint ends.

## Invocation Expectations

1. Define the sprint question and success criteria.
2. Read existing workspace context before adding new material.
3. Capture sources with `capture-source`.
4. Distill new notes with `distill-note`.
5. Connect material with `connect-thread`.
6. End with `session-closeout`.

## Rules

- Keep scope narrow.
- Prefer local workspace evidence over broad unsourced synthesis.
- Do not claim web research was performed unless it actually was.
